Spaghetti Bolognese

What is Spaghetti Bolognese?

Bolognese sauce is an Italian dish made up of a meat-based tomato sauce served with spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 good sized handful fresh parsle
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 pound spaghetti
  • Grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese for serving(optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and saut√© until lightly golden, 2 to 3 minutes. Add the garlic and fry another minute
  2. Add the ground beef
  3. Fry until fully cooked and no pink shows at all in the meat. Then add the tomato paste and mix with the meat. Add the oregano. Brown the meat well.
  4. Add the tomatoes, parsley, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer 15 minutes. Serve with freshly cooked spaghetti.
